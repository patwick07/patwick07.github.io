Events

The Conference Room

The resort offers meeting room facilities. Conference rooms can accommodate 50-60 participants. Its rooms are air-conditioned and set up with a basic sound system and projector providing an excellent venue for training, meetings, and more.

The Pavilion Hall

The Pavilion hall can house 300 guests. This room is ideal for large group events like catering, debut, and a lot more.

The Event Center

The Event Center is the biggest among the venues. It was boasting of an immense space that has 500 seating capacity. This venue will surely fit for your big events.
